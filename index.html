Munform

A to-the-point digital content management system for Model UN Conferences.

About

Munform is an adaptable online platform for Model UN Conferences. The goal of Munform is to resolve logistical issues wisely, without instilling additional burdens on conference participants. To this end, it is a highly customizable system that adapts to the variety of styles of Model UN globally.

Features

Controlled Access

Access to specific types of documents can be controlled so that the conference maintains a clear and accessible interface while protecting delegates' documents.

Approval Panel Integration

Munform can provide members of the approval panel access to correct resolutions, while providing centralized information about resolution correction deadlines.

Implementation with Google Docs

In conferences where Google Docs is primarily used, Munform can store resolutions without any overhead of tranferring documents between formats.
